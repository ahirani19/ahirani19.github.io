About Me

A quick map on some stuff you may have saw earlier...

If you paid attention to some of the objects on my landing page, here’s where you can learn a bit more about why they’re there!

metrocard

i am a new york city native (and therefore iron-clad with bagels and metrocards). i also take pride that spiderman is from my hometown (forest hills, queens).

bookshelf

slight offshoot, but i love all kinds of art and media, from graffiti to books to music. my favorite pieces in no particular order are the bear (tv show), almond by won-pyung sohn (book), and the amazing spiderman 2 (movie).

red backpack

i am a huge nature person. i like to run and lift on a regular basis, but prefer kayaking, hiking, mountain biking and swimming (and that red backpack is indeed my real life hiking bag).

ipad & pencil

my love of design largely comes from my love of visual art and animation. i pursued fine arts in high school as a hobby, and still draw to this day.

fun fact: every graphic on this page was drawn and animated by me!

arduino

robots and robotics have to be some of the coolest things to me, and i like to build and mess around with arduinos from time to time. i also love keeping myself up-to-date on robotics news and hope to work with some at a future job.

assorted coffee cups

there’s a 90% chance you will find me with a coffee in my hand at any given time, and a 99% chance that the coffee i’m drinking is a latte.

built with lots of indie rock and iced lattes
